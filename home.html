About

Computing for Community is a student-led club at Amity Regional High School, established in 2024 with a mission to leverage programming and software development skills for community service. Our members are proficient in a variety of programming languages, including Python, JavaScript, and C. We use these skills to create software solutions tailored for classrooms, non-profits, and local organizations. Our goal is to improve operational efficiency, enhance user experiences, and address the specific needs of these organizations. By bridging the gap between technology and community service, we aim to make a meaningful impact through innovation and collaboration.
